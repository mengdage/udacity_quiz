Saarloos Wolfdog

The Saarloos wolfdog, or Saarloos wolfhound is an established breed of dog originating from wolfdog hybrid crosses.

  • Dutch Name: Saarlooswolfhond
  • Average Length: 76 cm
  • Average weight: 45 kg
  • Colours: wolf-grey, red and white

They move lightly on their feet and have an elegant march. Its coat is short and dense, providing good protection from the weather. The Saarloos has wolf-like expressions, as well as a wolf-like head.
